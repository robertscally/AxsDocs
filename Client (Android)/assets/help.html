Capture an image

Capture an image by pressing the Take Photo button on the home screen.

Wait for the photo to be sent to the server to be processed.

Change Background Colour

When the text is returned from an image. It will be displayed on the screen.

Swipe from left to right to change the background colour.

Change Text Colour

When the text is returned from an image. It will be displayed on the screen.

Swipe from right to left to change the text colour.
